Crayon is a tool for plotting designs with characters and emojis in 2 dimensions, designs that can be copied and pasted as text.

It's been made after initially making plot, and while it's not as charming it is slightly better built.

It's also been built as a way to help me understand developing web apps with increasing complexity from scratch.

This is v1.0.0, further developments before v2.0.0 will include mobile functionality
